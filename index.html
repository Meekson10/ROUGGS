Rooted in Nature

A Sustainable Legacy

At ROUGGS, we believe true luxury respects its origins. Our atelier is dedicated to sourcing organic, undyed fibers and utilizing botanical dyes that return safely to the earth.

Every piece is crafted slowly, emphasizing human touch over machine speed. From raw linen harvesting to the final hand-stitched hem, our process is a dialogue with nature.
