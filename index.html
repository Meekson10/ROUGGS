The Atelier

A Legacy of Craftsmanship

At ROUGGS, we do not simply manufacture garments; we engineer heirlooms. Founded on the uncompromising principles of master tailoring, our atelier represents the pinnacle of sartorial elegance.

Every piece is conceived with an obsessive attention to detail, utilizing only the rarest, most sustainable materials sourced from historic European mills. Our artisans spend upwards of 80 hours hand-stitching a single jacket to ensure a silhouette that defies the passage of time.
