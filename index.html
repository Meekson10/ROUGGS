The Atelier

A Century of Excellence

For over a century, ROUGGS has dressed visionaries, magnates, and royalty. We represent the absolute zenith of luxury tailoring, where compromise is entirely absent from our vocabulary.

Our Master Tailors operate from our historic London atelier, utilizing exclusively sourced vicuña, heavyweight silks, and gold-spun threading. To wear ROUGGS is to inherit a legacy.
